General & Laparoscopic Surgery

Appendix Surgery

Appendix Surgery (Appendectomy)

Appendix surgery, or appendectomy, is the surgical removal of the appendix when it becomes inflamed or infected. We specialize in laparoscopic (keyhole) surgery, which uses small incisions and a camera for high precision. This modern approach ensures minimal tissue trauma, significantly reduces the risk of post-operative infections, and allows patients to return to their normal daily routine much faster than traditional open surgery methods.

Laparoscopic & Open Surgical Options
Minimal Post-Operative Pain & Scarring
Faster Recovery and Quick Hospital Discharge
Comprehensive Post-Surgery Care & Follow-up
Gallbladder Removal

Gallbladder Removal (Cholecystectomy)

Cholecystectomy is the surgical procedure to remove the gallbladder, primarily used to treat symptomatic gallstones and biliary conditions. Using advanced laparoscopic technology, we perform "Key-hole" surgery that offers superior precision. Our surgeons ensure safe duct identification and removal with minimal blood loss. This procedure is highly effective in providing permanent relief from gallbladder pain and digestive issues with a very high success rate and safety profile.

Advanced 4K Laparoscopic Visualization
Same-Day or 24-Hour Discharge Possible
Highly Precise Gallstone Clearance
Minimal Incisions for Superior Cosmetic Results
