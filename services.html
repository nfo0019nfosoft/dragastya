About Our Surgical Department

Dr. Agastya is a highly skilled General & Laparoscopic Surgeon dedicated to delivering safe, advanced, and minimally invasive surgical care. With expertise in keyhole procedures, he focuses on reducing pain, minimizing hospital stay, and ensuring faster recovery for every patient.
